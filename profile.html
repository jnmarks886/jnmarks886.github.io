My Profile

Jessica studied Business at Wake Forest University. After gradustion, she accepted an offer to join Jabil's Emerging Leader Rotational program, where she rotated between assignments in Jabil's Revenue Operations team. At Jabil she gained exposure to the complexities involved in building and bringing to market complex technologies. Her desire to sit more on the front end of innovation led her to join Corporate Executive Board (CEB), a research and advisory company serving 85% of Fortune 500, as a product management analyst. Through promotion, she moved over to Corporate Strategy, where she developed a more comprehensive of view of the business. Her passion for development and execution, led her to once again be promoted back to product development and management, where she launched prodcuts for CEB's fastest growing business segments. Jessica attended Kellogg in 2014-2015, and after graduation, she will join TripAdvisor's Product Management Rotational Program.
