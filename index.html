My Year at Kellogg

From Academics, to Clubs, and Travel, Get a Student Perspective on Kellogg's One Year Program

Welcome!

1Ys take Ice Bucket Challenge

My name is Jessica Marks, and I am a candidate for the Kellogg School of Management's One-Year (1Y) MBA program for the Class of 2015. The One-Year Program is very fast-paced, but it has all of the benefits of a two-year program, consolidated into one year. I joined the program in June of 2014, and I will be graduating in June of 2015.

Life at Kellogg is a little different for a 1Y student, so I created this website for both perspective and admitted students to learn more about the 1Y program.
